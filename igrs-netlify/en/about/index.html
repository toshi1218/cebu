Our Mission

Immigration attorneys shouldn't have to spend their time navigating Philippine government bureaucracy. IGRS exists to handle that entire process — so your firm can focus on what you do best: representing your clients.

We built IGRS as a bridge between US immigration law firms and the Philippine government document ecosystem, providing a reliable, professional, and transparent service that attorneys can depend on.

Our History

  • 2020

    Founded in Japan

    IGRS Corporation (株式会社IGRS) was established on June 1, 2020, headquartered in Wakayama City, Japan. Founded with the vision of becoming a trusted bridge between Japanese and Philippine business ecosystems.

  • 2020–2023

    Building On-the-Ground Expertise

    Established the Cebu City operational office and built direct working relationships with PSA, NBI, LTO, and DFA government offices. Developed internal processes for reliable document procurement and quality control, handling cases for initial business clients.

  • 2024

    B2B Service Launch

    Launched our structured BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) service offering, formalizing document procurement packages with standardized pricing, weekly reporting, invoice billing, and DHL international delivery. Began serving immigration law firms and registered support organizations.

  • 2025

    BPO Expansion & US Market Entry

    Expanded BPO operations and launched English-language services targeting US immigration law firms. Over 80% of our client base is now B2B, serving legal professionals handling Philippine nationals across multiple visa categories.

PH

Cebu Office

Cebu City, Philippines

Our on-the-ground team in Cebu City handles all direct government office interactions. They visit PSA, NBI, LTO, and DFA offices in person — the only way to reliably obtain these documents. Local expertise means faster processing and the ability to handle edge cases that online or remote services cannot.

  • PSA, NBI, LTO, DFA office visits
  • In-person document procurement

  • Quality verification before shipping

Why US Law Firms Trust IGRS
