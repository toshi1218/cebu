What to Include in Your Inquiry

To get an accurate quote quickly, please include the following details:

  1. Document types needed e.g., PSA Birth Certificate, NBI Clearance, CENOMAR, DFA Apostille, Translation
  2. Number of cases / individuals How many beneficiaries or sets of documents do you need? Volume discounts apply for 10 or more cases.
  3. Visa or case type e.g., K-1 fiancé visa, CR-1 spouse visa, H-1B, naturalization — this helps us recommend the right document bundle.
  4. Preferred delivery timeline Standard delivery is 4 weeks. Let us know if you have an earlier deadline and we will advise on feasibility.
  5. Delivery address Your firm's address for DHL international delivery.

Not sure exactly what documents you need? Just describe the case type and we will guide you on the standard document requirements.
