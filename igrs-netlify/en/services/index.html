PSA Certificates

Philippine Statistics Authority — Official Civil Registry Documents

What Are PSA Certificates?

PSA (Philippine Statistics Authority) certificates are the official civil registry documents issued by the Philippine government. These are the only documents accepted by USCIS, US consulates, and US immigration courts as proof of vital events for Philippine nationals. PSA documents are printed on security paper with anti-fraud features and bear the PSA seal.

All PSA documents procured by IGRS are obtained directly from PSA offices or authorized PSA Serbilis outlets in the Philippines. We do not use photocopied or digitally-reproduced documents.

NBI Clearance

National Bureau of Investigation — Philippine Police Clearance Equivalent

What Is NBI Clearance?

NBI (National Bureau of Investigation) Clearance is the Philippines' national-level background check document, equivalent to a police clearance or criminal background check. It is issued by the Philippine Department of Justice's National Bureau of Investigation and certifies that the individual has no pending criminal cases or derogatory records in the Philippines.

USCIS and US consulates routinely require NBI Clearance for Philippine nationals in immigration applications. It is considered more comprehensive than local police clearances because it covers the entire Philippines, not just a specific city or municipality.

NBI Clearance

The NBI Clearance must be current (typically within 6 months) at the time of submission. IGRS procures the clearance directly from NBI offices and can process apostille authentication for international use.

For applicants who have never registered with NBI or whose records require in-person verification, we handle the complete process including biometric enrollment.

LTO Driver's Records

Land Transportation Office — Driver's License Records

What Are LTO Records?

LTO (Land Transportation Office) records are official documents issued by the Philippine Land Transportation Office certifying a person's driving history and license status in the Philippines. These records are relevant to immigration cases where a driving history or license conversion is part of the application or supporting documentation.

DFA

DFA Apostille

Department of Foreign Affairs — Hague Apostille Authentication

What Is DFA Apostille?

The DFA (Department of Foreign Affairs) Apostille is the Philippines' implementation of the Hague Convention Abolishing the Requirement of Legalisation for Foreign Public Documents. The apostille is a standardized certification that authenticates the origin of a public document so it can be legally recognized in other countries that are party to the Hague Convention, including the United States.

USCIS and US consulates require Philippine documents submitted in immigration proceedings to be authenticated. Apostille is the accepted method for documents from the Philippines. Without apostille, PSA and NBI documents may be rejected as unauthenticated.

IGRS handles DFA apostille in-house as part of our document procurement pipeline, eliminating the need for law firms to coordinate a separate authentication step.

What's Included

Philippine PSA documents (birth certificates, marriage certificates, CENOMAR) are issued in English, but older documents or those with handwritten entries may require certified translation or transcription to ensure accuracy and readability for USCIS review.

Our translation service provides certified English translations of Philippine government documents including PSA civil registry records, LTO records, and other official documents. Translations include a certification statement attesting to accuracy and the translator's qualifications.
